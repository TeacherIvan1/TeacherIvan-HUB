Analyzing Line Graphs

Objective: Describe changes over time by alternating between Adjective+Noun and Verb+Adverb structures.

1. Academic Vocabulary: Degrees of Change

To describe a line graph, you must indicate *how fast* or *how slow* the line moved.

⚡ Sharply Bruscamente / Drásticamente Adjective: Sharp
🐢 Gradually Gradualmente / Poco a poco Adjective: Gradual
📏 Steadily De manera constante Adjective: Steady
🤏 Slightly Ligeramente Adjective: Slight

2. The Grammar Shift (GRA Requirement)

To score a Band 7.0 or higher in Grammatical Range and Accuracy (GRA), you cannot write "It increased sharply, then it dropped gradually" over and over again. You must demonstrate flexibility by shifting between two structures:

Structure A: Verb + Adverb
Subject + Verb + Adverb

Focuses on the action.

"Downloads increased sharply in March."
"The numbers fell gradually."
Structure B: Adjective + Noun
There was a + Adjective + Noun

Focuses on the existence of the change.

"There was a sharp increase in downloads."
"There was a gradual fall in the numbers."
🚨 THE PREPOSITION TRAP

When you use the Noun form (increase, decrease, rise, fall), you MUST use the preposition IN to talk about the subject.

❌ There was a sharp increase OF downloads.
✅ There was a sharp increase IN downloads.

Model Answer Analysis: The App vs. Exam Stress

Let's look at a line graph covering the first quarter of 2026 in Sucre. It compares the daily active users of the "Teacher Ivan - Lifestyle" app with the stress levels of philosophy students at Colegio Fielding.

The provided line graph compares the number of active users on a mobile lifestyle application with the reported stress levels of high school students over a three-month period from January to March 2026.

Overall, it is evident that both metrics experienced upward trends by the end of the period. However, while the application's user base grew steadily, the students' stress levels exhibited more dramatic fluctuations.

In January, the lifestyle app began with 500 active users. This figure rose gradually throughout February as the HTML interface was updated. By the end of March, there was a sharp increase in users, reaching a peak of 2,500.

Conversely, student stress levels started at a low 20% in January following the holidays. However, as the midterm philosophy exams approached in February, there was a sudden surge in anxiety, jumping to 80%. After the exams concluded, stress levels dropped slightly, but remained high at 75% in late March. Interestingly, domestic pets in the area, such as cats, maintained a steady plateau of zero stress throughout the entire quarter.
