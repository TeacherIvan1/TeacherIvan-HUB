2. B2 Grammar Cheatsheet

The Perfect Tenses
• Pres. Perf. Continuous: Duration/Sweat (have been + ing).
• Past Perfect: The "Time Machine" before another past action (had + V3).
• Future Perfect: Deadlines with BY (will have + V3).
Habits & Adaptation
• Used to + Base: A finished past habit.
• Get used to + ING: The process of adaptation.
• Be used to + ING: Normal, no longer a problem.
B2 Traps & Nuances
• The "A" Rule: "A few" is positive. "Few" is negative.
• Gerunds/Infinitives: Stop to smoke (pause to do it) vs Stop smoking (quit).
• State Verbs: Know, like, want NEVER take "-ing".
