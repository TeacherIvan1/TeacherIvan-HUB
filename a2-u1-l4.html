2. Memory Hacks & Pronunciation

🧠 NO RULES, JUST MEMORY

You cannot add "-ed" to these verbs. If you say "I goed to the park" or "I eated pizza", people will not understand you. You must memorize their past form.

Teacher Ivan's Memory Tricks

The "Number 8" Trick
The past tense of EAT is ATE. It is pronounced exactly the same as the number EIGHT (8)!
The "Silent GH" Trick
The past tense of BUY is BOUGHT. Ignore the "gh". Just pronounce it like "Bot" (/bɔːt/).
The Twin Verbs
Some verbs just drop one letter. MEET becomes MET. It's short and fast!
