2. Key Grammar: To Be (I am / You are)

The verb "To Be" means "ser" o "estar". Today we will learn the first two forms used to introduce ourselves and talk to others.

PronounVerb "To Be"Example
I (Yo)amI am Ivan. (Yo soy Ivan)
You (Tú / Usted)areYou are a student. (Tú eres un estudiante)

Let's see it in context:

Teacher Ivan: Good morning! I am a teacher at Colegio Fielding. Who are you?

Sarah: Hello! I am Sarah. You are my English teacher.
