Project: My Best Trip

Objective: Write a script and record a video/audio telling a story about a past holiday.

1. Your Mission 🚀

You survived the Past Simple! Now, it's time to prove it. Your final project is to create a 45-second presentation describing a trip you took in the past. You must use regular and irregular verbs, time expressions, and at least one negative sentence.

📝 Step 1: Write your story

Read Teacher Ivan's example below. Notice how the events are organized in chronological order.

"Last month, my girlfriend and I traveled to La Paz. First, we went to a big CrossFit competition. The altitude was exhausting, but the event was amazing! Then, we visited some friends and ate delicious food. We didn't stay in a hotel; we slept at a friend's house. Finally, we returned to Sucre. Perlita the cat didn't travel with us; she stayed at home."
🗣️ Step 2: Pronunciation Check

Practice your reading out loud. Remember the pronunciation of your -ed endings and don't forget the "Magic Eraser" rule for your negative sentences!

🎥 Step 3: Record your experience

Use your phone to record a video or a voice note. Imagine you are telling this story to your classmates at Colegio Fielding.

2. My Draft (Mi Borrador)

Write your story here. Organize your ideas before you start recording.

3. Final Checklist & Submission

Before you submit your project, make sure you used the grammar correctly!
