2. Key Grammar: Yes / No Questions

To ask a question in English using the verb "To Be", we just need to switch the order of the person and the verb.

You are a student. ⇄ Are you a student?
Question (Pregunta)Short Answer (Respuesta Corta)
Am I a teacher? Yes, you are. / No, you are not.
Are you a doctor? Yes, I am. / No, I am not.
Is he an engineer? Yes, he is. / No, he is not.

Let's see it in context:

(A stranger enters Colegio Fielding in Sucre looking confused)

Teacher Ivan: Excuse me, are you a teacher here?

Stranger: No, I am not. I am a doctor. Is this the hospital?

Teacher Ivan: No, it is not! This is a school. You are in the wrong place!
