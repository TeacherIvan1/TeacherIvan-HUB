Intro to Task 1: Describing Data

Objective: Understand the core structure of an IELTS Task 1 report and master academic trend vocabulary.

1. Academic Vocabulary: Trend Verbs

In IELTS, you cannot just say "go up" or "go down". You need to use advanced, precise verbs to describe graphs.

🚀 Skyrocket Dispararse (Subir rápido) Synonym: Surge
📉 Plummet Desplomarse (Bajar rápido) Synonym: Plunge
〰️ Fluctuate Fluctuar (Subir y bajar) Synonym: Vary
➖ Plateau Estancarse / Nivelarse Synonym: Remain stable

2. The 3-Part IELTS Structure

Task 1 requires you to write a summary of at least 150 words in 20 minutes. To get a high band score (7.0+), you must follow a strict, logical structure. Do not write a single continuous paragraph!

Paragraph 1

Introduction
Paraphrase the prompt. Write exactly what the graph shows, but use your own words. Do not copy the instructions.
"The provided line graph illustrates..." (Instead of: "The graph shows...")

Paragraph 2

The Overview
The most important paragraph! Summarize the 2 or 3 main trends. NO NUMBERS HERE. Just the big picture.
"Overall, it is clear that while X increased, Y experienced a downward trend."

Paragraph 3 & 4

Body Paragraphs
Support with data. Now you must zoom in. Compare the categories and use specific numbers, percentages, and dates from the graph.
"In 2024, the figure skyrocketed to exactly 50 hours, before plummeting to..."
🚨 THE FATAL IELTS ERROR

Task 1 is a REPORT, not an essay. You must NEVER give your personal opinion, guess why a trend happened, or use the word "I".

❌ I think sales plummeted because of the pandemic. (Automatic Band 5.0!)
✅ Sales plummeted significantly in 2020. (Just report the data).

Model Answer Analysis: Energy vs. Sleep

Imagine a line graph comparing the hours Teacher Ivan spends doing CrossFit per week, versus the hours Perlita the cat spends sleeping. Read the Band 8.0 model below.

[Introduction] The provided line graph illustrates the number of hours allocated to physical training by Teacher Ivan, compared to the sleeping hours of his cat, Perlita, over a one-week period in Sucre.

[Overview] Overall, it is abundantly clear that the two subjects experienced opposite trends. While the human's activity levels fluctuated throughout the week, the feline's resting hours remained consistently high and eventually plateaued at a maximum level.

[Body Paragraph] Looking at the details, Ivan began the week on Monday with a moderate 2 hours of CrossFit. This figure surged to a peak of 4 hours by Wednesday, likely due to a competition preparation. However, immediately after this peak, his training hours plummeted to zero on Thursday as he took a rest day.

[Body Paragraph] Conversely, Perlita’s sleeping pattern showed almost no variation. She began the week sleeping for an astonishing 16 hours. Although there was a slight dip to 15 hours on Tuesday, her resting time quickly recovered and plateaued at exactly 17 hours from Friday to Sunday, showing a complete lack of physical exertion.
