2. Grammar Quick Review

To Be in the Past
• Was: I, He, She, It. (I was happy).
• Were: We, You, They. (We were tired).
• Negative: wasn't / weren't.
Action Verbs (+)
• Regular: Add -ed (walked, visited).
• Irregular: Memorize them! (go ➔ went, eat ➔ ate, buy ➔ bought).
The Magic Eraser (-) & (?)
• Negative: Didn't + Base Verb
(I didn't go).
• Questions: Did + Subject + Base Verb
(Did you stay?).
Time & Sequence
• Time: Last week / Two days ago.
• Sequence: First ➔ Then ➔ After that ➔ Finally.
