Time Expressions

Objective: Specify exactly WHEN a past action happened using time markers.

1. Vocabulary: The Past Calendar

Learn these expressions. We usually put them at the end of the sentence.

📅 Yesterday Ayer
🌙 Last night Anoche
📆 Last week La semana pasada
⏳ ... ago hace ... (tiempo)

2. Grammar: The "Last" vs "Ago" Rule

To talk about when something happened, we use specific formulas. Pay attention to the position of the words!

Keyword Formula Example
LAST LAST + Time Period I traveled to La Paz last month.
AGO Number/Time + AGO I traveled to La Paz two months ago.
IN / ON IN + Year
ON + Day		 She moved in 2020.
We met on Friday.
🚨 THE SPANISH TRANSLATION TRAPS!

Do not translate word for word! At Colegio Fielding we strictly correct these two errors:

Trap 1: The invisible "The"
I played tennis the last week. ➔ I played tennis LAST WEEK.

Trap 2: The reversed "Ago" (Hace)
I arrived ago two days. ➔ I arrived TWO DAYS AGO.
