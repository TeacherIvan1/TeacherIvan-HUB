2. Grammar: Advanced Time Connectors

In B1, you used "Since" and "For". In B2, we upgrade these words to add emotional emphasis to your story of consistency. These connectors are perfectly paired with the Present Perfect Continuous.

Ever since
A stronger, more dramatic version of "Since". It emphasizes that the action has never stopped.
"Ever since I moved to Sucre, I have been training hard."
For as long as I can remember
Used when a habit feels like it has been part of your entire life.
"I have loved philosophy for as long as I can remember."
🚨 The "Ever Since" Formula Trap

When you use "Ever since" followed by an action, that specific action MUST be in the Past Simple because it is the trigger event. The second part of the sentence takes the Present Perfect.

❌ Ever since I have started teaching, I have been happy.
✅ Ever since I STARTED teaching, I have been happy.

Reading Context: Looking Back at March 2026

"It is March 2026, and looking back at my life in Sucre, I realize the power of consistency. Ever since I started at Colegio Fielding in 2021, I have been teaching my students the importance of philosophy and critical thinking. Throughout the years, I have seen them grow immensely."

"My girlfriend and I have a similar mindset. From day one of our relationship, we have been committing to our CrossFit routines. It is a lifestyle. That is why I have been developing the 'Teacher Ivan - Lifestyle' app to help others."

"And then there is Perlita. For as long as I can remember, she has been avoiding any form of physical activity. Ever since we adopted her, her only true consistency has been sleeping on the sofa!"
