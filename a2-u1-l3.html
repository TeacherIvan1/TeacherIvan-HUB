2. Grammar: The Spelling Rules

For Regular Verbs, the past is the same for ALL subjects (I, You, He, She, We, They). However, the spelling changes depending on the last letter of the verb.

The Pronunciation Secret 🗣️

How do we say the "-ed"? It depends on the sound before it. Never say "ed" unless the verb ends in T or D!

/t/ Like a fast, soft click. (After sounds like P, K, S, SH, CH) Walked, Stopped, Looked
/d/ A deep vibration in your throat. (After vowels and voiced sounds) Traveled, Enjoyed, Arrived
/id/ An extra syllable! ONLY if the base verb ends in T or D. Visited, Needed, Started
