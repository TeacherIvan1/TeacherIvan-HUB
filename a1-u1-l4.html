Spelling Names

2. In Context: "How do you spell that?"

When someone doesn't understand your name, they will ask:
"How do you spell your name?" or "How do you spell that?"

To answer, you say the letters one by one.

Let's see it in context:

Secretary: Welcome to Colegio Fielding! What is your name?

Student: My name is Mateo Quiroga.

Secretary: How do you spell your last name?

Student: It is Q - U - I - R - O - G - A.

Secretary: Thank you, Mateo. And who is your teacher?

Student: Teacher Ivan. I - V - A - N.
