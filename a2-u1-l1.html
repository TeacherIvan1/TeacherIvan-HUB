2. Key Grammar: Past Simple (To Be)

In Level A1, you learned "I am" and "You are" (Present). Now, we use Was and Were to talk about states or locations in the Past.

PronounsAffirmative (+)Negative (-)Question (?)
I / He / She / It was was not (wasn't) Was he...?
We / You / They were were not (weren't) Were they...?
🕰️ TIME MARKERS

When we talk about the past, we usually include specific "Time Markers" at the end of the sentence to show when it happened.

Yesterday (Ayer)
Last night (Anoche)
Last week (La semana pasada)
In 2020 (En el año 2020)

Let's see it in context: A Sunday in the City

Sarah: Teacher Ivan, where were you yesterday afternoon?

Teacher Ivan: I was in the Historic Center of Sucre. The weather was beautiful!

Sarah: Were you alone?

Teacher Ivan: No, I wasn't. My girlfriend and I were at the Central Market buying some food, and then we were at the Main Square.

Sarah: Was Perlita the cat with you?

Teacher Ivan: No, she wasn't! She was at home, sleeping on my computer keyboard!
