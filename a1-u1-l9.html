2. Grammar Quick Review

To Be: Affirmative (+)
• I am (I'm)
• He / She / It is (He's)
• We / You / They are (They're)
To Be: Negative (-)
• I am not (I'm not)
• He / She / It is not (isn't)
• We / You / They are not (aren't)
To Be: Questions (?)
• Am I...?
• Is he / she / it...?
• Are we / you / they...?
Wh- Questions
• What is your name?
• Where are you from?
• Who is she?
