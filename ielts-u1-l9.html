2. The Task 1 Ultimate Cheatsheet

Structure & Rules
• Paraphrase: NEVER copy the prompt. Use synonym surgery.
• The Overview: Summarize main trends. NO NUMBERS ALLOWED.
• No Opinions: This is a report. Never guess *why* a trend happened.
Data & Trends
• Grammar Shift: Alternate between Verb+Adv (fell sharply) and Adj+Noun (a sharp fall IN).
• Fractions: Use a third, a quarter, the vast majority.
• Tables/Bars: Find the extremes (highest/lowest). Avoid "shopping lists".
Maps & Processes
• Processes: Use Present Simple Passive (is constructed, are evaluated) + Sequencers (Subsequently).
• Maps: Use demolished, converted into, and to make way for. Watch your prepositions (in/to the north).
