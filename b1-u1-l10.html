1. Your B1 Mission 🚀

It is time to put everything together. Your final project for Unit 1 is to create a 60-second presentation about your fitness journey or a healthy hobby. You must use the "Zoom In" technique (Present Perfect ➔ Past Simple) and give advice to your classmates.

📝 Step 1: Read the Master Example

Look at my example below. Notice how I mix Present Perfect (Blue), Past Simple (Orange), and Power Words (Underlined).

"Hello everyone! I want to talk about my fitness journey. I have done CrossFit since 2022. (Zooming in...) I started training in a small gym in Sucre because I wanted to be healthier. My girlfriend and I have already competed in La Paz, but we haven't gone to an international event yet. Last year, I pulled a muscle, so I had to rest for two weeks. You should find a sport you love, and you must rest when you are tired. It is the best way to relieve stress!"
