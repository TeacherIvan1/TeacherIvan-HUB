Level A1 • Unit 1 • Final Project

Project: Present yourself

Objective: Write a script and record a short video/audio introducing yourself in English.

1. Your Mission 🚀

It is time to speak! Your final project for Unit 1 is to create a 30-second presentation. You must use the Verb To Be to talk about your name, country, profession, and one extra detail (like an interest or a pet).

📝 Step 1: Write your script

Look at my example below, then write your own presentation.

"Hello! My name is Ivan. I am from Sucre, Bolivia. I am a teacher at Colegio Fielding. In my free time, I am a CrossFit athlete. I have a cat. Her name is Perlita. She is very small! Thank you."
🗣️ Step 2: Practice

Read your script out loud 3 times. Focus on the pronunciation of "I am" and "is".

🎥 Step 3: Action!

Use your phone to record a video or a voice message. Don't be shy, mistakes are part of learning!
