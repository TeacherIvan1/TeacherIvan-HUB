In the Classroom

Objective: Learn classroom objects and use "This" and "That" to point at things.

1. Vocabulary: Classroom Objects

Listen and repeat the objects you can find in your classroom.

🖊️ Pen Bolígrafo / Lapicero
✏️ Pencil Lápiz
📓 Notebook Cuaderno
🎒 Backpack Mochila
🪑 Desk Escritorio / Pupitre
🏫 Board Pizarra

2. Key Grammar: This vs. That

We use This and That to point at singular objects (only one thing).

WordMeaningWhen to use it?
THIS Este / Esta / Esto For an object that is NEAR you (close to your hands). CERCA
THAT Ese / Esa / Aquel For an object that is FAR from you. LEJOS

Let's see it in context:

(In a classroom at Colegio Fielding)

Teacher Ivan: Look at my hands. This is my pen. And look at the wall over there... that is the board.

Student: Teacher, is this my notebook?

Teacher Ivan: No, that is Sarah's notebook. Your notebook is in your backpack.
