2. Grammar Quick Review

Present Perfect Rules
• Unfinished Time: Have/Has + V3. (Today, this week).
• Experiences: Have you ever...? I have never...
• Zoom In: Use Present Perfect for the general idea, and Past Simple for specific details.
The Power Words
• For vs Since: For + Number (for 3 days). Since + Date (since 2021).
• Position: Just / Already go in the middle. Yet goes at the END.
• Tickets: Been = Return. Gone = One-way.
Passive Voice & Modals
• Passive: Verb To Be + V3. Focus on the object (The games were held).
• Modals: Should (advice), Must (strong rule), Have to (external obligation). NEVER use "to" after Should/Must.
