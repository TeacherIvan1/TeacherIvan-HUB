Unit 1 • Lesson 3

Where are you from?

Objective: Learn countries, nationalities, and plural forms of the verb "To Be".

1. Vocabulary: Countries & Nationalities

Listen and practice the pronunciation of these places and their nationalities.

Bolivia 🇧🇴 Bolivian (Boliviano/a)
The United States 🇺🇸 American (Estadounidense)
The UK 🇬🇧 British (Británico/a)
Brazil 🇧🇷 Brazilian (Brasileño/a)

2. Key Grammar: To Be (Plurals)

When we talk about more than one person (plurals), we always use the verb ARE.

PronounVerb "To Be"Example
We (Nosotros)areWe are students.
You (Ustedes)areYou are from Sucre.
They (Ellos/Ellas)areThey are teachers.

Let's see it in context:

Teacher Ivan: Good morning class! We are in Sucre. Are you from Bolivia?

Students: Yes, we are Bolivian. We are students at Colegio Fielding.

Teacher Ivan: Excellent! Look at those tourists outside. They are from the USA. They are American.
